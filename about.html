// what is turnerhacks

Peel Region's
Biggest Student
Hackathon.

TurnerHacks is a 24-hour sprint where the boldest student builders in Brampton, Mississauga, and beyond come to turn ideas into reality. Hosted at Brampton City Hall — it's more than a competition. It's a movement.

No experience required. Just ambition.

// who we are

Students
Building for
Students.

We're a crew of high school students from the Peel District School Board. TurnerHacks was born out of frustration with the lack of local hackathon opportunities — so we built one ourselves.

Our team handles everything: sponsorships, venue logistics, workshops, and making sure every hacker walks away with something real.
